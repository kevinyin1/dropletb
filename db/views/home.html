DONATE BLOOD TODAY

One donation can save up to three lives

Each year, 4.5 million people die due to the lack of blood for transfustions. For each donation you can save up to 3 lives. About one pint is given in each donation.

You can safely donate your whole blood every 8 weeks, plasma every 28 days, automated red cells every 16 weeks and platelets every 3 days up to 24 times each year.

Although an estimated 38 percent of the U.S. population is eligible to donate blood at any given time, less than 10 percent of that eligible population actually do.
